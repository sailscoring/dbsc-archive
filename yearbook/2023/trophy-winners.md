# Trophy Winners 2022 — DBSC Yearbook 2023

Transcribed from the class pages of the DBSC 2023 Yearbook.

- **Source:** <https://www.dbsc.ie/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/DBSC_2023_-yearbook-download.pdf>
- **Pages:** 31–42 (of 47)
- **Method:** text extracted from the PDF's text layer (2026-07-16), with page
  renders used to resolve pages carrying stale hidden text under the artwork
  (Cruiser 0). Verbatim, including the yearbook's own typos, marked `(sic)`.

Most class pages head their table "Trophy Winners 2022", but **four pages
carry a different printed year** — Glen and PY "2021", Dublin Bay 21 "2021",
IDRA 14 "2020" — noted per class below. Where such a table conflicts with the
same year's table in another edition, both are recorded verbatim in their own
edition's file; we do not resolve which season the data actually belongs to
(see repo rule on modelling discrepancies).
