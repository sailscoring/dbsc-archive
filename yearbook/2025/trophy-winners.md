# Trophy Winners 2024 — DBSC Yearbook 2025

- **Source:** <https://issuu.com/irelandafloatmagazine/docs/dbsc_yearbook_2025>
- **Pages:** 33–45 (of 48)
- **Method:** manual transcription from screenshots of the Issuu viewer
  (2026-07-15); see `../README.md`. Verbatim, including the yearbook's own
  typos, marked `(sic)`.

Winners 2024"; Melges 15 has an undated "Series winners"). Columns are trophy,
series won, boat, and winner(s), as printed; a blank cell is blank in the
yearbook.
