# Trophy Winners 2025 — DBSC Yearbook 2026

Transcribed from the class pages of the DBSC 2026 Yearbook.

- **Source:** <https://issuu.com/irelandafloatmagazine/docs/dbsc_2026_yearbook_>
- **Pages:** 36–48 (of 52)
- **Method:** manual transcription from screenshots of the Issuu viewer
  (2026-07-15); see `../README.md`. Verbatim, including the yearbook's own
  typos, marked `(sic)`.

Each class page heads its table "Trophy Winners 2025" (some "DBSC Trophy
Winners 2025"; Melges 15 has "Series winners 2025"). Columns are trophy,
series won, boat, and winner(s), as printed.
