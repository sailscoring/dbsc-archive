# Trophy Winners 2020 — DBSC Yearbook 2021

Transcribed from the class pages of the DBSC 2021 Yearbook.

- **Source:** <https://issuu.com/irelandafloatmagazine/docs/dbsc_2021_>
- **Pages:** 22–27 (of 33)
- **Method:** manual transcription from screenshots of the Issuu viewer
  (2026-07-16); see `../README.md`. Verbatim, including the yearbook's own
  typos, marked `(sic)`.

Each class block heads its table "Trophy Winners 2020" (some "DBSC Trophy
Winners 2020"; Squib has an undated "Class Trophies"). This was the shortened
COVID season — a shared "Cut Glass Tumbler" Tuesday trophy appears across
several classes. Cruiser 4 and Mermaid have no class block in this edition's
class pages, and Dublin Bay 21 has no trophy table.
