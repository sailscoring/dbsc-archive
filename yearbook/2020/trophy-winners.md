# Trophy Winners 2019 — DBSC Yearbook 2020

Transcribed from the consolidated awards pages of the DBSC 2020 Yearbook.

- **Source:** <https://issuu.com/irelandafloatmagazine/docs/dbsc_2020_yearbook>
- **Pages:** 35–37 (of 45)
- **Method:** manual transcription from screenshots of the Issuu viewer
  (2026-07-16); see `../README.md`. Verbatim, including the yearbook's own
  typos, marked `(sic)`.

This edition is structured differently from later ones: the class pages
(pp. 22–34) carry only a "Club Trophies" inventory (trophy names and what they
are awarded for — no winners; not transcribed here), and the winners are
published centrally as **"Premier Awards 2019"** and **"Major Trophies 2019"**
tables. Those tables print trophy | class | boat | winner (the Premier Awards
add a citation column), and are transcribed below.
